Sunrise Cafe serves breakfast, beverages, snacks, and desserts. 
For breakfast, the menu includes Masala Omelette priced at 120 rupees, Veg Sandwich priced at 90 rupees, and Butter Toast priced at 60 rupees.
Beverages available are Filter Coffee for 50 rupees, Cappuccino for 120 rupees, and Masala Chai for 40 rupees.
Snacks offered include Veg Puff priced at 40 rupees, Samosa priced at 35 rupees, and French Fries priced at 100 rupees.
Desserts on the menu are Chocolate Brownie for 150 rupees and Vanilla Ice Cream for 90 rupees.
All items are freshly prepared and prices include 18% taxes with as final bill.
Breakfast service is available until 11:30 AM.
